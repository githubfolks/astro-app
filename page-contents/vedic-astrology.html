☸️ What is Vedic Astrology?

Vedic Astrology, or Jyotish (the science of light), is the world's most ancient and sophisticated system of understanding human destiny through the celestial clockwork.

Unlike Western systems, it uses the sidereal zodiac and places immense importance on the Nakshatras (lunar mansions) to provide a surgical level of precision in timing life's major milestones.
