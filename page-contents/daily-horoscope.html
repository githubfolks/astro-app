🌞 What is a Daily Horoscope?

A daily horoscope is a snapshot of the celestial landscape, mapping the transits of planets against your zodiac sign. It provides a unique lens through which to view your day's challenges and opportunities.

By understanding the Moon's transit and planetary alignments, you can better navigate emotional tides and choose the most auspicious moments for action.
