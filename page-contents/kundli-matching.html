🕉️ What is Kundli Matching?

Kundli Matching, also known as Guna Milan or Horoscope Matching, is the time-tested Vedic practice of comparing the celestial blueprints of two individuals before they enter the sacred bond of marriage.

At its core, it utilizes the Ashta-koota system, which evaluates 8 different aspects (Kootas) of compatibility, resulting in a total score out of 36 Gunas. This analysis considers the positions of the Moon and other planets to determine the spiritual and physical synchronization between the couple.
