PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

At Aadikarta (accessible at aadikarta.org), protecting the privacy of our seekers and astrologers is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy details the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the security measures we implement to protect it.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
To provide accurate astrological guidance and consultations, we collect:
- Account Registration: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
- Astrological Birth Details: Date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth (used for generating Kundli reports, compatibility charts, and personalized daily horoscopes).
- Chat & Consultation History: WebSocket-based live chat logs and transcripts between seekers and astrologers (stored securely to generate AI-assisted consultation summaries, review history, and handle quality audits/disputes).
- Financial Transactions: Wallet recharge history, top-ups, and payout details. We do not store credit card or bank account details; all transactions are processed via secure, PCI-DSS compliant third-party payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay).
- Technical Data: IP address, device type, operating system, browser specifications, and connection logs (used for billing engine synchronization and network drop-off detection).

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
Your data is used strictly to run and optimize the Aadikarta platform:
- Core Services: Connecting you with verified astrologers and calculating live chat duration for per-minute or package billing.
- Personalization: Generating custom daily/weekly planetary transits, auspicious timings (Muhurta), and personalized horoscope feeds.
- AI Enhancements: Creating automated post-session summaries and pre-consultation astrological briefs for astrologers.
- Communications: Sending transaction alerts, wallet low-balance warnings, referral notifications, and booking reminders.
- Legal & Compliance: Generating tax invoices (including Indian GST) and auditing transaction history.

3. DATA RETENTION AND PROTECTION
- Confidentiality: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal details or consultation history to any third parties.
- Encryption: All live chat transmissions via WebSockets and database storage are protected using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption.
- Astrologer NDA: All verified astrologers on Aadikarta are bound by strict non-disclosure agreements regarding seeker details.

4. THIRD-PARTY INTEGRATIONS
We utilize trusted third-party services for specific platform operations:
- Payment Processing: Razorpay (for secure credit, debit, UPI, and wallet payments).
- Video & Audio: MiroTalk SFU (for live group webinars or video consultations).
- Analytics: Standard tools to monitor platform performance and crash logs.

5. SEEKER RIGHTS & CONSENT
By registering an account and using the Aadikarta platform, you consent to the collection and processing of your birth and contact details as described here. You can update your profile details at any time or request account deletion by contacting support@aadikarta.org.
